# Privacy Policy for DailyVault

**Last Updated:** July 11, 2026

DailyVault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the DailyVault application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.** 

DailyVault is designed as a strict, offline-first application. All data you enter into the app—including notes, reminders, documents, and shopping lists—is saved locally on your device using local storage mechanisms (such as IndexedDB and LocalStorage).

## 2. Data Storage and Security

Because your data never leaves your device, you maintain full ownership and control over your information. We do not have servers that store your data, and we do not sync your data to any cloud service. 

To ensure your data remains secure:
* Ensure your device has a strong password, PIN, or biometric lock.
* Only install applications from trusted sources.

## 3. Third-Party Access

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks that collect data about your usage. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with anyone, simply because we do not possess it.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app is completely offline, any changes to this policy will only reflect updates to our operational practices (which currently involve zero data collection). We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
